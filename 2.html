PC Knowledge

Building Your First PC

Start with selecting compatible components: CPU, motherboard, RAM, storage, PSU, and case. Ensure proper cooling to avoid overheating.

Troubleshooting Common Issues

If your PC won't boot, check power connections and try resetting CMOS. For slow performance, run a malware scan and defrag your drives.

Upgrading Tips

Upgrade RAM for better multitasking or add an SSD for faster load times. Always back up data before making changes.
